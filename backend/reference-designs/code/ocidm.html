Your First Visit

We encourage everyone to seek regular dental care. Dr. Bensimon will develop a specific plan for your oral health needs.

Your first appointment will generally last about an hour and a half. This covers a full dental examination with digital x-rays if necessary.

Each person has unique needs. Our team will go over all treatment plans and options with you to help you decide the best treatment possible.
