Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist? expand_more

It is generally recommended to visit the dentist for a check-up and cleaning every six months. However, depending on your oral health needs, our dentist in Hamilton may suggest more frequent visits.

Do you offer sedation options? expand_more

Yes, we offer sedation dentistry options, including nitrous oxide, to help anxious patients feel relaxed and comfortable during their procedures.

Are you accepting new patients? expand_more

Yes! Centre Mall Dental is always welcoming new patients and families from Hamilton and surrounding areas. Contact us today to schedule your first appointment.

What do I do in a dental emergency? expand_more

If you have a toothache or emergency, call our clinic immediately. We prioritize emergency cases to get you out of pain as quickly as possible.
